About Me

I'm a full-stack and platform engineer with 4+ years at Delta Air Lines, where I own both application code and the cloud infrastructure it lives on. Whether the need is backend, frontend, infrastructure, or a production issue that needs resolving fast — I'm the person the team turns to.

My engineering practice is shaped by the craftsmanship principles of Robert C. Martin ("Uncle Bob") and Martin Fowler — SOLID principles, clean architecture, and thoughtful refactoring — so systems built to move fast stay maintainable over time.

AI is an active, daily part of that practice. I use Amazon's Kiro agentic IDE with live MCP server integrations on real production work, build generative-AI applications with Spring AI and LangChain/LangGraph, and deepen ML/DL expertise through an ongoing M.S. in Artificial Intelligence.
