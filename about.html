About Me

I'm a full-stack and platform engineer who builds production-ready software and uses AI as a force multiplier, not a shortcut. My background spans financial services (American Express) and airline operations (Delta Air Lines) — both as a contractor who had to earn trust fast, deliver under constraints, and hand off systems that kept running. That's how I naturally work.

Technically, I live across the full stack: Java, Spring Boot, Quarkus, TypeScript, Angular, Python, Kubernetes, AWS, GitLab CI, Argo CD, and Grafana in production daily. On the AI side, I build with Spring AI, LangChain, LangGraph, RAG pipelines, and MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers — and I use Claude and Amazon Kiro as part of my daily engineering workflow.

What drives me most is putting AI to work on problems that matter — in nonprofits, public service, education, health, and community organizations. I'm deepening that work through an ongoing M.S. in Artificial Intelligence. If you're building at the intersection of software, applied AI, and social impact — let's connect.
